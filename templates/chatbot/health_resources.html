⚠️ When to Seek Urgent Care

Seek Urgent Care (ASAP) if you experience:
Difficulty Breathing

Shortness of breath, wheezing, or labored breathing

Chest Pain

Pressure, pain, or discomfort in chest

Severe Injuries

Deep cuts, fractures, or significant trauma

Sudden Weakness

One-sided weakness or numbness

Confusion/Altered Mental Status

Disorientation or unusual behavior

Severe Allergic Reaction

Swelling, difficulty breathing, or severe rash

Severe Abdominal Pain

Sudden, intense pain with vomiting

High Fever with Stiff Neck

May indicate meningitis

🚨 CALL 911 IMMEDIATELY if:
  • Difficulty breathing or choking
  • Severe chest pain or pressure
  • Loss of consciousness
  • Severe bleeding
  • Signs of stroke (face drooping, arm weakness, speech difficulty)
  • Poisoning or overdose
  • Any life-threatening emergency

🩹 Basic First Aid Tips

For Minor Cuts & Wounds
  • Wash the wound with clean water
  • Apply direct pressure with a clean cloth if bleeding
  • Apply antiseptic or antibiotic ointment
  • Cover with a clean bandage
  • Change bandage daily
  • Watch for signs of infection
For Burns
  • Cool the burn with cool (not cold) water for 10-20 minutes
  • Remove jewelry or constrictive items
  • Apply antibiotic ointment
  • Cover with a clean gauze bandage
  • Take over-the-counter pain reliever if needed
  • Don't apply ice directly to skin
For Sprains & Strains
  • RICE Method:
  • Rest - Avoid activity that causes pain
  • Ice - Apply ice for 15-20 minutes, several times daily
  • Compression - Wrap with elastic bandage
  • Elevation - Raise the injured limb
  • Over-the-counter pain relievers may help

💪 Wellness & Prevention

Healthy Lifestyle Habits
  • Get 7-9 hours of quality sleep daily
  • Exercise at least 150 minutes per week
  • Eat a balanced diet with fruits, vegetables, and whole grains
  • Stay hydrated - drink plenty of water throughout the day
  • Limit alcohol consumption
  • Don't smoke - it damages your health
  • Manage stress through meditation or relaxation techniques
Preventive Health Measures
  • Get recommended vaccinations
  • Maintain regular check-ups with your healthcare provider
  • Practice good hand hygiene - wash frequently
  • Stay updated on health screenings
  • Manage chronic conditions carefully
  • Maintain a healthy weight
  • Monitor blood pressure and cholesterol regularly
Mental Health
  • Seek support when feeling overwhelmed
  • Maintain social connections
  • Practice self-care and relaxation
  • Consider professional counseling if needed
  • Limit social media and screen time
  • Engage in hobbies and activities you enjoy
  • Don't hesitate to reach out for mental health support

💊 Medication Safety

Safe Medication Use
  • Always take medications as prescribed by your doctor
  • Read all labels and instructions carefully
  • Keep track of all medications you're taking
  • Tell your healthcare provider about all medications and supplements
  • Store medications properly - follow storage instructions
  • Check expiration dates regularly
  • Never share medications with others
When Taking Medications
  • Take medications with water unless directed otherwise
  • Take at the same time each day for consistency
  • Don't stop taking medications without consulting your doctor
  • Report any side effects to your healthcare provider
  • Be aware of potential interactions with other medications
  • Keep a list of all medications and supplements
  • Ask about generic alternatives to reduce costs
💡 Tip: Keep a medication list with you at all times. This helps healthcare providers understand your complete medical situation.
